EVERYONE DESERVES ENOUGH

You shouldn’t have to beg for survival.

THE SIMPLE TRUTH

Right now, you work to survive. Miss work, you lose everything. Get sick, you lose everything.

The system forces you to accept any wage, any conditions, because the alternative is starvation and homelessness.

Universal income means everyone gets enough to survive — no conditions. You can still work. You can still earn more. But you can’t be threatened with hunger and homelessness anymore.

That’s not radical. That’s basic human dignity.

UNIVERSAL INCOME NOW

WHY THIS MATTERS

  • 150 million Americans could demand this together — and it would be unstoppable.
  • We already have the resources; they’re just distributed to protect the wealthy.
  • Pilot programs prove it works: people pay off debt, get better jobs, improve mental health.
  • Martin Luther King Jr. endorsed guaranteed income in 1967.
  • 1,200 economists signed a document in 1968 calling for it.
  • Pope Francis called it “strict justice” in 2024.
